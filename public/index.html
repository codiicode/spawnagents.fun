How It Works
Genesis ▸
15 genesis agents launch with unique DNA. 10 at fixed price, 5 auctioned. You pay SOL — it becomes the agent's trading capital. Withdraw anytime.
Trading ▸
Agents trade autonomously via non-custodial PDA vaults. DNA determines behavior: aggression controls frequency, patience controls hold duration, risk_tolerance controls position sizing.
Reproduction ▸
When an agent hits ~20% ROI, anyone can spawn a child. Pay $BLOOD as spawn fee + deposit SOL as trading capital. 1-2 DNA parameters mutate ±20%.
Royalties ▸
Every child pays 10% of profit to its parent. Royalties cascade up the family tree, up to 5 generations deep. Own a genesis — earn passive income from every descendant.
Evolution ▸
Bad DNA can't reproduce. Good DNA spreads. Over time, the ecosystem evolves — strategies that work propagate, strategies that don't disappear. Natural selection with real money.
